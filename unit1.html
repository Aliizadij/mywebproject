Q 1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects –ts? How does GDPR impact digital project.
GDPR gives data subjects like online services users, a high degree of control via data processing. It expands their goals and rights, because it gives them better insight into the quantity of information has been stored by users. This tells how information is used and let them to make a restriction and even a completely stop in case. GDPR that stands for the General Data Protection Regulation has come into effect since 2018. Every individual has to work under 8 regulations of GDPR which definitely impact our projects so business.
